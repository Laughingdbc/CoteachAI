Ready to Create Your Own TEACH Prompts?

Now that you've explored our collection of ready-to-use TEACH framework prompts, it's time to create your own! Remember to include all five components: Task, Example, Audience, Context, and How to Format for the best results.

TEACH Framework Quick Reference:

  • T - Task: Start with a clear action verb that defines what you want the AI to do.
  • E - Example: Provide models, frameworks, or samples to guide the output style.
  • A - Audience: Define who the content is for and any specific needs they have.
  • C - Context: Give background details to make the response relevant to your situation.
  • H - How to Format: Specify the structure and organization you want for the response.
